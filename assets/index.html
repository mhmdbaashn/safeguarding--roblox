Who is Responsible For Safeguarding ?

It is everyone’s responsibility to protect children and young people and keep them safe. It is important that families, communities and professionals can recognise when a child or young person is being harmed and that they know what action to take in response.

The Dangers of Roblox ?

The Roblox boom is giving some parents pause, as they wonder if the game's multiplayer format and free and open communication policy are safe for kids. As with any video game with these features, there are some risks, but overall, Roblox can be a safe gaming platform for older children according to experts—especially when parents take some precautions. Here, we dive into what Roblox is, potential risks you should know about, and how you can keep your child safe from the risks experts want all parents to know about.
